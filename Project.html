Flood Hazard Assessment Map

Analysis Results: The flood hazard index in Riau Province is generally in the high zone, although there are some areas in the low zone. The method used is based on BNPB Regulation No. 2 of 2012 and other guidelines from national-level ministries/agencies. The map results show the distribution of points from patrols corresponding to the flood hazard categories in the raster data. This indicates the alignment of patrol results with field calculations using specific methods.
